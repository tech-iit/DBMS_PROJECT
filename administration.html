  Administrators are the people who control the Hospital Management System (HMS). Administrators have the authority to view the 
  personal details of the Patients, Employees as well as the Doctors. 
  They have the permission to delete or add any entry in any of the declared lists with the accordance of the 
  Director of the Hospital.

  They are able to view the submitted feedbacks by the patients. And can forward any necessary feedbacks to the director 
  of the hospital.
