    Patients are requested to Sign In to the HMS, and their personal data will be stored within the system, which can be accessed
    by the Doctors and Staff of the Hospital. After being Logged in to the system, they can attain the medical services provided 
    by the medical staff of the hospital.
    The Attending Doctor will make a report based on the evaluation of the Patient. And the report will be made available to patient
    and can be downloaded as soon as the Doctor gives his consent.

    The Patient will have the facility to report the feedback based on a staff member assigned to him/her or the doctor.
    The medical report will contain Bills and details of the diseased state of the patient.
