    Employees represent the Staff in any hospital, who are delegated to assist a Doctor on any of his surgeries or 
    any of his patients. The Staff needs to be well manerred, as a feedback form is generated which is optional 
    and can be filled by any Patient and Doctors. Unexpected behaviour will be discouraged with deduction in their salaries
    and a stern warning.

    They too are able to see the medical reports of a Patient, but are not allowed to edit them.
    Instead they can approach the Doctor with their suggestions, which if the Doctor feels worthy, he can change the medical 
    report.
